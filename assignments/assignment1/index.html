New Jersey

New Jersey is a great state, here is some information everyone should know:

The capital of New Jersey is Trenton and it was the 3rd ever state in the United States. It's nickname is "The Garden State" because of the surplus of food grown there in the late 1800s. The state bird is the Eastern Goldfinch. New Jersey is a great place to live because it offers many different types of areas to live in.

  2. Countryside
  3. City
